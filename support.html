Common questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the ClearBride app → Settings → Account → Manage Subscription → Cancel. Your access continues until the end of the billing period. You can also email us and we'll handle it for you.
I subscribed on the web — how do I activate access in the app?
Sign in to the app with the same email address you used to subscribe. Your access activates automatically within a few seconds of signing in. If it doesn't, tap Settings → Account and wait a moment for the subscription check to complete.
My partner isn't seeing my updates in real time.
Both devices sync every 30 seconds and immediately when you open the app. Make sure your partner is signed in with their own account (not yours) and that they accepted the partner invite. If the issue persists, email us with both account email addresses.
I lost my data after reinstalling / clearing app storage.
All your data is saved to your account in the cloud — it's not stored on the device. Simply sign back in with your email and password and everything will be restored. If you're still missing data after signing in, contact us immediately.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently deletes all your wedding data. This action cannot be undone. You can also email us and we'll take care of it for you.
Is my wedding data private?
Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data or share it with advertisers. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
Something in the app isn't working right.
Use the feedback button inside the app (Settings → Send Feedback) to report a bug — it includes your device info automatically. Or email us directly at clearbride@gmail.com. We ship fixes fast.
